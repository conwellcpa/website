A Candid Note

We're a strong fit for some, and the wrong choice for others

We keep our client list deliberately small so the planning stays personal. Honesty up front saves everyone time.

You'll likely get real value if…

  • You own a business or carry meaningful, complex income
  • You want a partner who plans ahead, not just files
  • You value judgment and access over the lowest fee
  • You're thinking in years, not just this April

We're probably not your firm if…

  • You need a single, simple W-2 return at the lowest price
  • You only want to be contacted once a year
  • You're looking for aggressive, off-the-shelf "loopholes"
  • Price is the only thing that matters to you
