Home / Advisory

Advisory is the whole point. The return is just the receipt.

For business owners and high-income individuals, the difference between a good year and an expensive one is rarely the preparer's software. It's whether someone was thinking three moves ahead.

Compliance asks, "what happened?" Advisory asks, "what should happen next, and what will it cost or save?" We spend most of our time on the second question.

Where the Value Is

The decisions we help you get right

Each of these is worth more than the cost of preparing a return — and each is gone if it isn't addressed in time.

Entity & structure

Whether to elect S-corp status, when a holding company earns its keep, and how to structure multiple ventures cleanly.

Owner compensation

The split between salary and distributions, sized to be both tax-efficient and defensible if questioned.

Income timing

Accelerating or deferring income and expenses across years to keep more of it in lower-taxed brackets.

Retirement & deferral

Solo 401(k), defined-benefit, and SEP plans used as the tax tools they are, not afterthoughts.

Real estate & investments

Depreciation, passive activity rules, and how rental and investment income interact with the rest of your picture.

Transition & exit

For founders heading toward a sale, the multi-year groundwork that's far cheaper to lay before the deal is on the table.

The Planning Cycle

Why "year-round" is more than a slogan

Q1

Close the prior year, set the baseline

Filing & baseline
Q2

First checkpoint
