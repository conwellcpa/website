Questions

Frequently asked

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes. The initial 30-minute consultation is completely free with no obligation. It's a chance for us to understand your situation and for you to decide if we're the right fit.

Do you work with clients in other states?

Absolutely. With our secure online portal and e-signature, we serve clients wherever they are. Federal returns are the same nationwide, and we handle multi-state filings regularly.

How much do your services cost?

Individual returns start at a flat fee, and business clients typically work on a flat monthly package. We always quote you up front after the consultation — no hourly surprises.

I'm behind on bookkeeping and past returns. Can you help?

Yes — catch-up and cleanup work is one of our specialties. We'll bring your books current and file any missing or amended returns to get you back on track.

How do I send you my documents securely?

After onboarding, you'll get access to an encrypted client portal where you can upload documents, sign forms, and message us securely — no emailing sensitive files.
