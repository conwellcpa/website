Before You Reach Out

Questions we're often asked

What happens on the first call?

We talk through your situation — your business, structure, and what's prompting the search — and give you an honest read on whether we can genuinely help. There's no cost and no obligation, and if we're not the right fit, we'll say so.

How do you charge?

Engagements are quoted as a flat fee after we understand your situation — annual for individuals, monthly for businesses. You'll always know the number before any work begins, and routine questions never restart a meter.

Do you work with clients in other states?

Yes. With a secure portal and e-signature, we work with clients wherever they're based, and we handle multi-state filings routinely.

Can you take over from my current accountant?

Often, yes — transitions are common and we manage them so nothing slips. We'll review your recent returns and books as part of the diagnostic before anything changes.

I'm behind on books or past filings. Is that a problem?

Not at all. Cleanup and catch-up are a normal starting point. We'll bring things current and put a structure in place so they stay that way.
