Three disciplines, run as one engagement

Strategy, compliance, and accounting are usually split across vendors who never talk to each other. We hold all three — so the planning is grounded in real numbers and nothing falls between the gaps.

Discipline 01

Tax Planning & Strategy

The work that actually lowers your liability — done in advance, modeled against real numbers, and revisited as your year changes.

  • Multi-year projectionsWe model this year and the next several, so today's decisions account for tomorrow's brackets.
  • Entity & compensation structureS-corp elections, reasonable salary, and owner-pay strategy sized to your business — not a rule of thumb.
  • Retirement & deferralSolo 401(k), defined-benefit, and SEP strategy to move income into lower-taxed years.
  • Quarterly reviewsEstimates and timing adjusted through the year so April is a confirmation, not a reckoning.

Discipline 02

Tax Preparation & Compliance

Precise, defensible returns — the execution layer that carries out the strategy and stands up to scrutiny.

  • Individual & business returns1040s with equity, K-1s, and rentals; 1120-S, 1065, and 1120 for your entities.
  • Multi-entity & multi-stateCoordinated filings across holding and operating companies and the states you touch.
  • Reviewed, not rushedEvery return is checked line by line before it's filed. Accuracy is the baseline, not the goal.
  • RepresentationIf a notice or examination comes, we stand in front of it on your behalf.
Discipline 03

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Advisory-grade books are not a clerical task — they're the instrument panel every good decision depends on.

  • Current, reconciled monthlyBooks closed on a schedule so you're never reconstructing a year in March.
  • Financials you can readStatements that tell you what's happening in the business, with context, not just totals.
  • Cleanup & structureWe'll bring a messy or behind set of books current and put a chart of accounts that makes sense in place.
  • Grounded planningBecause we keep the books, the strategy is built on numbers we've already verified.
