Services built around your goals

We focus on three core disciplines — bookkeeping, tax preparation, and tax planning — and offer a handful of supporting services to round out everything a small business needs.

Core Service 01

Bookkeeping

Accurate, up-to-date books are the foundation of every good financial decision. We keep yours clean so you always know where you stand.

  • Monthly reconciliationsBank, credit card, and loan accounts reconciled every month.
  • Financial statementsProfit & loss, balance sheet, and cash-flow reports you can actually read.
  • Cleanup & catch-upBehind on your books? We'll bring months (or years) current and keep them that way.
  • Always tax-readyBecause we keep the books, your return is faster, cheaper, and more accurate.
Core Service 02

Tax Preparation

Whether it's a straightforward 1040 or a multi-entity business return, we prepare it accurately, file it on time, and stand behind it.

  • Individual returnsFederal and state, including multi-state, self-employment, rentals, and investments.
  • Business returnsSole proprietorships, partnerships, LLCs, S-corps, and C-corps.
  • Every credit & deductionWe dig for what you're entitled to — and double-review every return before filing.
  • Prior-year & amendedCatching up on missed years or fixing an old return? We handle those too.
Core Service 03

Tax Planning

The biggest tax savings come from decisions you make before year-end — not at filing time. We plan ahead so April holds no surprises.

  • Year-round strategyQuarterly reviews to adjust withholding, estimates, and timing as your year unfolds.
  • Entity & compensationS-corp elections, reasonable salary, and owner-pay strategy to reduce self-employment tax.
  • Retirement & deductionsSEP/Solo 401(k), HSA, and timing of income and expenses to lower your bracket.
  • ProjectionsKnow your estimated liability ahead of time so you can set money aside with confidence.
