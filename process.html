You'll know exactly what working together looks like

No pressure, no mystery, and no engagement letter until both of us are confident it's the right fit. Here's the path from first conversation to long-term partner.

1

Strategy call

A focused, no-cost conversation about your business, your structure, and what's prompting the search. The goal is mutual clarity — including an honest read on whether we're the right firm for you.

~30 minutes · no cost
2

Diagnostic review

If it makes sense to continue, we review your recent returns, entity structure, and books. This is where the real opportunities — and any risks — come into focus.

We do the work · you share documents securely
3

Proposal & flat fee

You receive a clear scope and a flat fee — annual for individuals, monthly for businesses. You'll know precisely what's included and what it costs before you decide anything.

No hourly surprises
4

Onboarding

We set up your secure portal, gather what we need once, and put the foundation in place: clean books, a chart of accounts that fits, and a baseline projection.

Set up once, not every season
5

The plan in motion

Quarterly planning, current books, on-time filings — and direct access to the CPA who knows your file whenever a real decision comes up. This is the part most firms skip.

Year-round, not once a year
