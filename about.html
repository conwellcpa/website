Our Story

Built on relationships, not transactions

Christopher Conwell, CPA PC was founded on a simple belief: clients deserve a CPA who knows their name, returns their calls, and looks out for them all year — not just at tax time.

Too many people only hear from their accountant when a return is due. We do it differently. By keeping your books current and planning ahead, we turn tax season from a scramble into a non-event — and we find opportunities to save along the way.

Whether you're an individual, a freelancer, or a growing small business, you'll work directly with a licensed CPA who takes the time to understand your goals and explain your options in plain English.
